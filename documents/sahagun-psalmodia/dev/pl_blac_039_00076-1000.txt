
    Ma ocquezquilhuitl, maoc ximono-
notza, maxiccuepa in moiollo initech-
copa in tictemachtitinemi, auh maxic-
cuepa in motlatol, in motemachtil, yo\~a
in motlachiual, in motlaneltoquiliz: auh
inintlaço Dios in ſant Mathias, quinanqui
li, quilhui.
    Macamo iuhqni quimonequiltiz in
Dios, \~i niccaoaz in melaoac tlaneltoqui
liztli, in ieonimacoc, \~i oniquicnopilhui.
    Auh in iehoanti Iudiosme, ic oquimo
tetepachilihuique in iehoatzi ſant Mathi
as, auh yoan quimoquauiuitequilique.
